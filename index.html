WHAT WE DO

The aim of Allergy Pals is to provide a learning source for teachers who have or are expecting to have a student with food allergies in their classes. Including information on symptoms and treatments for the eight basic categories of foods that could cause allergies. Our hope is that they can better understand and handle the problems their students face.
